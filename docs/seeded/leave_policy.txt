Title: Leave Policy — doddle2dollars

Casual Leave
Employees are entitled to 12 casual leaves per calendar year. Casual leave cannot be carried forward to the next year. A maximum of 3 consecutive casual leaves can be taken at once. Prior approval from the reporting manager is required.

Sick Leave
Employees are entitled to 10 sick leaves per calendar year. A medical certificate is required for sick leave exceeding 2 consecutive days. Sick leave cannot be carried forward.

Earned Leave
Employees accrue 18 earned leaves per calendar year (1.5 days per month). Up to 30 days of earned leave can be carried forward to the next year. Encashment of earned leave is permitted at the time of separation.

Maternity and Paternity Leave
Female employees are entitled to 26 weeks of paid maternity leave. Male employees are entitled to 10 days of paid paternity leave. These are in addition to the casual, sick, and earned leave entitlements.

Leave Application Process
All leave requests must be submitted via the HR portal at least 2 working days in advance, except for emergencies. The reporting manager must approve within 1 working day. For sick leave taken without prior approval, notify your manager on the same day.
